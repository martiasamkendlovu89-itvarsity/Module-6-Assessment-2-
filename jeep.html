Exterior

The jeep features seven slot grille, flat fender flares and iconic round headlamps, as well as the uniquely wrangler features such as the removable doors, roof and easily foldable windshield.

The all-new Jeep Wrangler makes it even easier to experience open-air freedom thanks to its easy-to-fold windshield. Unlike previous models, the fold-down process requires the removal of far fewer bolts.

A new body-colour sport bar is also part of the permanent windshield frame structure, while redesigned hood bumpers feature integrated washer nozzles for a seamless appearance.

Hardtop, soft top, no top or the new Sky One-Touch Power Top, full doors or no doors, the all-new Jeep Wrangler is made for easy, open-air driving.

Technology

Our most advanced Uconnect systems to date allow you to stay in control and connected to the rest of the world.

All New Wrangler features a standard custom-configurable 7-inch multiview information digital cluster display that lets you tailor your personal settings and graphics. The full-colour display integrates detailed navigation features and media information, such as song, artist and station name, and also displays real-time transfer case status.

The cluster also features the Off-Road Pages, which provides the driver with a number of helpful gauges that pertain to off-roading, such as pitch, roll, altitude, GPS coordinates, drivetrain power distribution and more.

The Alpine Premium Audio System is available on the Sahara and Rubicon, with a 552-watt 12-channel amplifier, eight speakers and a rear subwoofer. The system is enhanced by active noise cancellation and acoustic laminated windshield glass.
